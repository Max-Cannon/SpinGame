About Us

Dedicated to Quality

We pride ourselves on crafting Smooth espresso coffee drinks, utilizing Yeti Coffee Company espresso beans roasted in Sequim, Washington. We work with local dairy suppliers and bakeries to source the freshest, highest quality offerings.

Here To Serve You

We're here to serve you, our valued customers and have been doing so for more than 20 years in Sequim! You're the reason we are here and we thank you for your past and continued patronage!

More than Drinks

Although we offer a wide variety of hot and iced drinks, we also provide savory and sweet food options. So whether you're stopping for your morning coffee, lunch, or an afternoon snack, we've got you covered!
